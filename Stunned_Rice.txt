For stunned rice (500Gr) preparation, is necessary the next ingredients:

1-) Rice. (200Gr)
2-) Differents meat's. (300Gr)
3-) Salt. (2 Teaspoons)
4-) Water. (200ml)
5-) Forget Oil (1 Teaspoons)

Now, the steps for preparation recipe is:

1-) Throw the rice in a metal container.
2-) Mix the oil with de rice.
3-) Pour the water into the container and leave it on low heat.
4-) Throw the meat's into the mix.
5-) Wait until the water is not visible.
6-) Comprobate the cooking of rice entering a knife (This should not be wet).
7-) Wait intro 10-15 minutes for service de rice.
